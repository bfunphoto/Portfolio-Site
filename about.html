Hi, I'm Brian Funderburke! I'm a freelance photographer and designer based in Lynchburg, Virginia. My work has been featured in Photographer's Forum Annual Photography Contest, the Popular page on 500px, as well as multiple different websites and social media accounts across the web. I've been shooting photography and designing since 2013, and have a Bachelor's in Graphic Design with a minor in photography. I have a passion for using photography, videography and design to capture the beauty in the world and hope your enjoy my work!
